Travel Diaries

Southeast India

  • Among all my travels, one of the most special and longest journeys I had was with my four friends from undergrad: Shiva, Pavan, Shinde, and Tarun. Our mentor, Mr. Manish, generously lent us his car for an epic road trip covering almost Southeast India. We traveled from Hyderabad to Kanyakumari, a trip of approximately 4000 km. The funny part is that only two of us, Pavan and Tarun, knew how to drive among the five of us! We started our journey on January 18th, 2024, and returned to Hyderabad on January 26th, 2024. For a detailed look at our itinerary, check out the video highlights on my Instagram.

Pondicerry

  • The second most memorable trip was to Pondicherry, which was my first trip with friends. I went with my best friend Badhri and his two friends, Kiran and Sai Krishna. We explored almost the entire city of Pondicherry from September 22, 2022, to October 1, 2022. We had a fantastic time and enjoyed the trip immensely.

Anantha Giri Hills

  • Another memorable trip was a spontaneous plan to Anantha Giri Hills. It was a 36-hour road trip during the monsoon, where we had an incredible experience walking and trekking through the hills. One of the most special moments of this trip was spending a sleepless night sitting by the roadside, chatting with strangers, and asking for various things. It was a small but notable adventure that left a lasting impression.

Delhi

  • Delhi, the capital of India, is a city I’ve visited twice. The first time was when I was a child, so I don't remember much about it. The second time, I went with my family and my sister, and it was an "okay" kind of experience.
